Good to know
How much does pressure washing cost?
Most driveways run $129–$300 depending on size and condition; house soft washes start at $249. Use the sixty-second quote above for your exact range — the final price is confirmed on-site before we begin.
Is it safe for my roof and siding?
We never blast delicate surfaces. Roofs and siding receive a low-pressure "soft wash" with specialised detergents that kill mold and algae at the root — manufacturer-approved and more effective than pressure alone.
Are you licensed and insured?
Fully licensed and carrying $1M in liability coverage. You're protected from the moment we arrive.
How long does it take?
Most driveways take one to two hours; a full house wash two to four. We confirm timing when you book, and we leave the property tidier than we found it.
Do I need to be home?
For exterior work, no — provided we have access to water and the work area. We send before and after photographs when the work is complete.
