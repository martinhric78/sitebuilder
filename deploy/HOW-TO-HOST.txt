============================================================
  HOW TO GO LIVE IN 5 MINUTES — NETLIFY FREE HOSTING
============================================================

STEP 1: Sign up (free, no credit card)
  → Go to netlify.com → click "Sign up" → use your email

STEP 2: Upload this whole folder
  → In Netlify dashboard click "Add new site"
  → Click "Deploy manually"  
  → Drag the entire "deploy" folder onto the upload box
  → Wait ~10 seconds

STEP 3: You get a live URL instantly
  → Netlify gives you something like:
     https://sparkly-wave-482910.netlify.app
  → That's it. Everything is live.

STEP 4: Get a real domain (optional, $12/yr)
  → In Netlify: Site settings → Domain management → Add domain
  → Example: tampabaywebpros.com → buy on Namecheap.com
  → Point it to Netlify in 2 clicks

YOUR LIVE URLS:
  Dashboard:  yoursite.netlify.app/
  Builder:    yoursite.netlify.app/builder/
  JJ Demo:    yoursite.netlify.app/demos/jj-pressure-washing/

HOW TO ADD A NEW CLIENT DEMO:
  1. Open builder/ → fill in client info → Download
  2. Create a new folder in demos/ named after the client
     e.g.  demos/smith-painters/
  3. Put the downloaded HTML file in there, name it index.html
  4. Go to Netlify → Deploys → drag the updated deploy folder
  5. New URL is live: yoursite.netlify.app/demos/smith-painters/

SENDING A DEMO TO A PROSPECT:
  Text or email them the link, example:
  "Hey Mike, I built this for your business — take a look:
   https://yoursite.netlify.app/demos/jj-pressure-washing/
   No cost to look. Takes 30 seconds."
